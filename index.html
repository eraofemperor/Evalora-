About Evalora

Evalora is an innovative platform designed to simplify the evaluation of student answer sheets. Using state-of-the-art Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Evalora extracts text from handwritten or typed answer sheets, allowing teachers to evaluate answers efficiently and generate organized mark lists in Word or PDF formats.

How Evalora Helps Teachers

Save Time

Automate text extraction from answer sheets, reducing manual transcription and speeding up the grading process.

Improve Accuracy

Leverage advanced OCR to accurately interpret even poor handwriting, minimizing errors in text recognition.

Streamline Grading

Assign marks for specific questions, add comments, and generate downloadable mark lists in CSV or PDF formats.

How Evalora Benefits Students

Clear Feedback

Receive detailed marks and comments for each answer, helping you understand your performance and improve.

Organized Results

Access evaluation results in structured formats (Word, PDF), making it easy to review and track progress.
